Our Mission: To provide compassionate and effective counseling services to those seeking to achieve lasting wellness. We believe that every individual has the potential to overcome their challenges and live a fulfilling life, and we are committed to supporting our clients in their journey towards healing and personal growth.

WHY CHOOSE SECOND WIND?

Second Wind Behavioral Health Services provides comprehensive assessments and evaluations to identify the root causes of an individual’s mental health or substance abuse challenges. Our staff provides customized treatment plans that lead to overall wellness. These services may include psychological assessments, addiction assessments, medical evaluations, counseling services, and other useful resources that aid in positive changes in the lives of our clients..
